THE PROBLEM

Policy shapes every girl's life. Yet no one teaches her to read it.

Across India, millions of girls grow up under systems shaped entirely by policy decisions — education schemes, welfare access, safety laws, and institutional structures — while remaining excluded from understanding them.

Project Dastak exists to bridge that distance between governance and lived experience.

FOUNDER

Voice is a form of education.

Project Dastak was founded by Nandini Puri, a student of Mathematics and Economics at Lady Shri Ram College for Women, University of Delhi.

The project emerges from a simple belief: civic literacy should not remain restricted to privileged spaces.
