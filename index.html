Why Dastak Matters

Policy shapes every girl's life, yet no one teaches her to read it.

49%

Of Indian women have never participated in civic engagement.

1 in 3

Girls cannot identify a single government welfare scheme.

SDG 5

Ensuring full participation in political and public life.

Where it Begins

I arrived through a room full of children.

Every opportunity, every policy, and every scheme designed for these children had been built entirely without them.

Dastak is built on one belief: civic literacy is not a privilege, it is a right. A child, a girl who learns to read a policy document is finally inside the room.

Four Months. One Playbook.

August: Ground & Connect

Partnering with schools in South Delhi to map lived encounters with policy.

September: Decode

Translating dense government laws into plain, accessible language for students.

October: Amplify

Practising civic participation through petitions and guest speaker sessions.

Nov-Dec: Release

Publishing the open-source Playbook for other campuses to replicate.
